## Features of the Generated PDF

✅ **Professional Layout**
- Clean, organized design
- Clear section separation
- Easy to read typography

✅ **Complete Information**
- Your business details
- Client information
- Itemized services
- Automatic calculations
- Payment instructions

✅ **Ready to Send**
- PDF format (universally compatible)
- Professional appearance
- All necessary details included

## Customization Options

You can customize:
- Business name and contact info
- Client details
- Invoice number and dates
- Service descriptions
- Quantities and rates
- Tax rate
- Discount amount
- Payment methods
- Notes and terms

## File Location

Generated PDFs are saved to: `./data/exports/[invoice-number].pdf`

Example: `./data/exports/INV-001.pdf`
